The story

Born from a river, built on coffee

Galani took its name from the River Gelan, which runs from the Bale mountains down to Arba Minch, crossing many of Ethiopia's great coffee regions. In those areas, coffee beans are carried on the backs of donkeys — and that journey inspired both the name and the Galani logo.

Galani Coffee was born from the passion and need to share Ethiopia's extraordinary coffee with a wider public. We are in the birthplace of this magic seed — and we only wish to offer our friends and customers the real taste of Ethiopia, well roasted and well served.

As Moplaco's consumer-facing brand, Galani proves what our beans are capable of at their best — in the cup, in the community, in the classroom. Everything served at Galani is sourced, processed, and roasted by the Moplaco family.

"We believe in quality. Quality is not an accident but the result of hard work that allows us to set the bar high and mark the detail that sets us apart."

Coffee education

Galani Learning — SCA Certified Training

Training is what pushes all of us to understand and treat coffee as it deserves — professionally and with respect. Galani offers SCA-certified courses from beginner all the way to professional level, taught on Moplaco's own lots.

Introduction to Coffee
Perfect for those starting their coffee journey. Covers origins, varieties, processing methods, and basic brewing fundamentals using Ethiopian coffees.

Barista Skills
Hands-on espresso technique, milk steaming, workflow, and sensory evaluation. Designed for aspiring baristas and café professionals.

Brewing & Filter Methods
